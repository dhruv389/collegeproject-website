PN Junction Characteristics Experiment

Aim:

The aim of this experiment is to study the characteristics of PN junction diode and to determine the forward and reverse biases.

Apparatus:

  • PN junction diode
  • Power supply
  • Multimeter
  • Connecting wires

Procedure:

  1. Connect the PN junction diode to the power supply and multimeter as shown in the circuit diagram.
  2. Apply the forward bias to the PN junction diode and measure the forward current and forward voltage.
  3. Apply the reverse bias to the PN junction diode and measure the reverse current and reverse voltage.
  4. Plot the characteristics curve of the PN junction diode.
