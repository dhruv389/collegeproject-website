Efficiency of Solar Cell Experiment

Aim

To determine the efficiency of a given solar cell.

Apparatus

  • Solar cell
  • Voltmeter
  • Ammeter
  • Power supply

Procedure

  1. Connect the solar cell to the voltmeter and ammeter using the appropriate connections.
  2. Connect the power supply to the circuit to provide a known voltage to the solar cell.
  3. Record the readings of the voltmeter and ammeter.
  4. Calculate the efficiency of the solar cell using the formula: Efficiency = (Power Output / Power Input) * 100
