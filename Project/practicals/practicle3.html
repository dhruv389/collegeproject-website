Measuring the Divergence of a Laser Source experiment

Aim:

To measure the divergence of a given laser source.

Apparatus:

  • Laser source
  • Screen
  • Ruler
  • Protractor

Procedure:

  1. Project the laser beam onto a screen placed at a certain distance from the laser source.
  2. Measure the width of the laser beam on the screen using the ruler.
  3. Use the protractor to measure the angle of divergence of the laser beam.
  4. Calculate the divergence of the laser source using the formula: divergence = (width of beam) / (distance from laser source to screen) x (tan of angle of divergence).
