Zener Diode Characteristic Experiment

Aim:

To study the characteristic of a Zener diode and determine the Zener voltage and breakdown voltage.

Apparatus:

  • Zener diode
  • Power supply
  • Multimeter
  • Resistor
  • Connecting wires

Procedure:

  1. Connect the Zener diode, resistor, and multimeter in a circuit as shown in the diagram.
  2. Set the power supply to a low voltage and gradually increase the voltage.
  3. Measure the voltage across the Zener diode and the current flowing through it for each step.
  4. Plot a graph of voltage versus current to obtain the Zener diode characteristic.
  5. Determine the Zener voltage and breakdown voltage from the graph.
