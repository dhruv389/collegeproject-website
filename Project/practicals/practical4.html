Experiments with Laser Sources and Ultra Sonic Sensors experiment

Aim:

To measure the distance dimension of an obstacle using a laser source.

Apparatus:

  • Laser source
  • Screen
  • Ruler
  • Protractor
  • Obstacle

Procedure:

  1. Project the laser beam onto the obstacle and onto a screen placed at a certain distance from the laser source.
  2. Measure the height of the laser beam on the obstacle using the ruler.
  3. Measure the height of the laser beam on the screen using the ruler.
  4. Use the protractor to measure the angle of incidence of the laser beam on the obstacle and the angle of reflection of the laser beam on the screen.
  5. Calculate the distance dimension of the obstacle using the formula: distance dimension = (height of beam on screen) x (tan of angle of incidence) / (tan of angle of reflection).
